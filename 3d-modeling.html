FAQ

Questions BIM managers ask us

Can you work to our BIM Execution Plan?
Yes. We build to your BEP, including LOD requirements, model origin, shared coordinates, worksets, and file naming. If you don't have a BEP, we can help draft one aligned to ISO 19650.
What levels of development (LOD) do you model to?
We routinely deliver LOD 200, 300, 350, and 400 across electrical, mechanical, structural, and architectural disciplines. LOD is defined in kickoff and verified at every milestone.
How do you handle clash detection?
Navisworks-based clash detection with weekly clash reports, categorized by severity and discipline. Resolution tracking integrates into BIM 360, ProjectWise, or your existing issue management system.
Can you build custom Revit families?
Yes. Parametric Revit families to your content standards, including MEP families with connectors, shared parameters, and type catalogs. We maintain a library history per client.
Do you create digital twins?
Yes. We create data-linked digital twins that integrate with CMMS, asset management, and facility operations systems. Deliverables include tagged equipment, room relationships, and maintenance-linked metadata.
