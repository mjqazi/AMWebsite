2026 offshore engineering rates report.

Planning-rate bands for electrical drafting, BIM modeling, solar permits, arc flash studies, and managed engineering documentation work.

How to use this report

These are planning bands for budgeting and vendor comparison, not guaranteed quotes. Scope quality, standards ownership, revision SLA, security, and engineering review usually move total project cost more than the lowest hourly rate.

The three pricing models

Engineering managers usually compare three models: direct offshore BPO, managed hybrid delivery, and local onshore consultancy. The cheapest hourly rate is rarely the cheapest package if the vendor cannot own standards, revision control, and QA.

Why hourly rates mislead

A $25/hr drafting vendor can cost more than a $65/hr managed team when every issue returns twice, every title block is interpreted differently, and your senior engineer spends Friday nights reviewing avoidable mistakes. For technical documentation, measure cost per accepted drawing, cost per permit approved, or cost per closed RFI.

Procurement checklist

  • Ask whether QA is included in the rate or billed separately.
  • Require sample drawings in your standards before issuing a large package.
  • Compare revision SLA, not only first-issue turnaround.
  • Confirm source-file ownership, NDA coverage, and storage controls.
  • Separate production drafting from stamped engineering judgement in the commercial model.
