FAQ

Questions civil managers ask us

What codes do you work to?
IBC 2021, ACI 318, AISC 360, ASCE 7-22 (US), AS 3600, AS 4100, AS/NZS 1170 (Australia), and Eurocodes for European jurisdictions.
Can you provide stamped drawings?
Yes. PE-stamped drawings are available in applicable US jurisdictions through our partnered PEs. We also partner with Chartered Engineers for Australian and UK projects.
How do you handle earthworks and volume calculations?
Cut and fill analysis using Civil 3D or MicroStation Power InRoads. Volume calculations, mass haul diagrams, and site balance optimization included.
Do you coordinate with architects and structural on multi-discipline projects?
Yes. We work in Revit and AutoCAD collaborative workflows with architects, MEP, and structural teams. Clash detection integrated into our deliverables.
