Methodology

How this calculator works

We benchmark your drawing counts against published rate data from major onshore engineering consultancies across the US, Australia, Canada, UK, and Europe. Our per-drawing rates are the averages we quote to new clients.

What it includes:

  • Average fully-loaded onshore drafter cost per drawing type
  • Advanced Mechanix standard per-drawing rate
  • Typical time-per-drawing benchmarks

What it excludes:

  • Revision cycles beyond the included standard (2 per drawing)
  • Specialist studies (arc flash, load flow, protection coordination)
  • Stamp fees where applicable
  • Onboarding and standards-library setup (one-off)

Calculator output is an estimate based on industry benchmarks, not a quote. Actual pricing depends on scope, complexity, and engagement model. Request a detailed scoping call for a firm proposal.
