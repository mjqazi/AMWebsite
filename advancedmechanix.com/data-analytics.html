§03 — The Execution Layer

Meet OptiScheduleAI — our sister platform for AI-powered industrial scheduling.

Predictive analytics tells you what to do. OptiScheduleAI does it. A multi-agent AI architecture that replaces manual scheduling with optimized output in minutes.

How the integration works

From insight to executed schedule — one workflow.

  1. Analytics surfaces the trigger. Predictive model flags a bearing failure risk, or efficiency analytics identifies a throughput bottleneck.
  2. OptiScheduleAI ingests the signal. Bidirectional SAP / CMMS sync pulls the maintenance order, work breakdown, and constraints.
  3. The AI schedules it. Billions of combinations evaluated, constraints auto-enforced. First-issue output passes 99%+ of the time.
  4. Disruption? Replan in 20 seconds. Drag-and-drop Gantt with real-time labor and cost impact feedback.

§05 — FAQ

Questions operations leaders ask us

What data sources can you integrate with?
Historians (PI, Wonderware, GE Proficy), SCADA systems, CMMS (Maximo, SAP PM), ERP, MES, and custom OT data sources. Read-only integration by default with client-controlled data egress. OptiScheduleAI adds bidirectional SAP sync for the scheduling layer.
How does the AI scheduling integration work?
Our analytics layer identifies what to optimize — a failure risk surfaced by a predictive model, a throughput bottleneck identified by efficiency analytics, a maintenance backlog pulled from the CMMS. OptiScheduleAI takes that signal and generates an executed schedule: optimized work orders, shutdown plans, or workforce allocations. The two layers share a common SAP / CMMS integration path. You can run them independently or as a combined stack.
How do you handle sensitive operational data?
NDAs, encrypted workspaces, role-segregated engineer access. We can run entirely inside your environment (VDI, cloud tenant) with no data leaving your network. All engineers bound by individual confidentiality agreements.
How long does a typical pilot take?
Analytics pilot: 4–6 weeks for data ingestion, one predictive model, and one operational dashboard. OptiScheduleAI scheduling pilot: typically under 60 days to first documented ROI, including integration with your SAP / CMMS and a shadow-mode validation period against your current scheduling process.
Do you build dashboards we can maintain internally?
Yes. We build on Power BI, Tableau, or Grafana with full knowledge transfer. Dashboards are yours to maintain, or we can operate them on retainer. OptiScheduleAI provides its own operator-grade Gantt interface plus dashboard export to your BI platform.
What outcomes are typical from analytics + scheduling together?
Analytics alone: 20–35% reduction in unplanned downtime within 6 months, 15–25% reduction in maintenance spend. Adding OptiScheduleAI execution: 20–50% workforce efficiency gain, shutdown-planning cycle reduced from weeks to minutes, and documented shutdown savings averaging around $1.8M per event across verified deployments. Combined stack compounds both.
Can we start with just analytics, then add scheduling later?
Yes — that's the most common path. Analytics pilot first validates the data foundation; once predictive models and dashboards are producing clear optimization targets, OptiScheduleAI scheduling is layered in to execute them. Each layer stands alone; combining them compounds the return.
