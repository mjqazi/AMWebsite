§03 — FAQ

Substation-design questions

What voltage classes do you cover?
Up to 500 kV for transmission substations; 33 kV, 11 kV, and 6.6 kV for distribution and industrial substations; 400 V / 480 V LV distribution.
Do you handle IEC 61850 substation automation?
Yes. SCD and ICD file review, IED configuration documentation, and station-bus architecture drawings for IEC 61850-compliant substations.
Can you do greenfield and brownfield substations?
Both. Greenfield: new substation SLD, physical layout, and protection design. Brownfield: as-built updates, protection re-setting documentation, and refurbishment drawings.
Do you produce client-specific standards?
Yes. We work inside utility-specific template libraries from the engagement start. Our teams maintain client-specific cell libraries, title blocks, and protection-panel conventions per active engagement.
What turnaround can we expect?
Simple distribution substations: 2–4 weeks for complete documentation. Transmission substations (132 kV+): 6–12 weeks depending on complexity and scope.
