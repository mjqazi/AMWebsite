FAQ

Questions electrical managers ask us

Which electrical codes do your engineers work to?
NEC 2023 (US), IEC 60364 and IEC 61439 (international), AS/NZS 3000 (Australia/NZ), CSA C22.1 (Canada), and BS 7671 (UK). Every deliverable is QA'd against the applicable jurisdiction standard as part of our standard process.
Can you work inside our AutoCAD or Microstation templates?
Yes. We match your title blocks, cell libraries, layer naming, pen tables, and drawing conventions exactly. We can also work inside your hosted environment via Citrix, VDI, or a client-managed VPN.
What is your typical turnaround on a single-line diagram?
3–5 business days for first issue on standard SLDs. Complex MCC or switchgear packages run 2–3 weeks. Revisions under our managed-service engagements are returned within 24 hours.
Do you perform arc flash and coordination studies?
Yes. We perform IEEE 1584-compliant arc flash studies, protection coordination, and short-circuit analysis using ETAP and SKM Power Tools. Reports are delivered with incident energy labels ready for field printing.
How do you handle revisions and change orders?
Project-based engagements include two revision rounds in the fixed price. Managed-service clients receive unlimited revisions with a 24-hour SLA. Any additional change orders are quoted in writing before work begins — no surprise invoices.
