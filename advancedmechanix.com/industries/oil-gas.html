§03 — Oil & Gas FAQ

Questions O&G engineering managers ask us

Do you handle API hazardous-area classification?
Yes. API RP 500 and API RP 505 compliant classification drawings, equipment schedules, and installation documentation for Class I Division 1/2 and Zone 0/1/2 environments typical in petroleum facilities. We coordinate with your HAZOP outputs and P&IDs.
Do you cover upstream, midstream, and downstream?
Yes. Wellhead and surface facility documentation for upstream; pipeline stations and compressor stations for midstream; refinery and petrochemical plant documentation for downstream. Each has specific code and operator standards we maintain libraries for.
Can you support LNG export terminal projects?
Yes. LNG electrical documentation including cryogenic heat tracing, low-temperature cable selection, and cold-climate equipment specification per IEEE 515 and relevant LNG standards.
Do you work with major EPCs on O&G projects?
Yes. We regularly support major EPC subcontractor workflows. Standard flow-down NDAs, IP clauses, and deliverable ownership apply; we work under the EPC's title block as part of their deliverable.
