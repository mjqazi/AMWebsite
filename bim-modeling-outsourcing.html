§05 — FAQ

BIM outsourcing questions we answer on day one

What does BIM modeling outsourcing include?
Revit production for electrical, mechanical, structural, and architectural disciplines; 2D to 3D conversion of legacy drawings; Navisworks clash detection with categorized reports; custom Revit family libraries; digital twin creation; and shop drawing generation from coordinated models.
How is your Revit outsourcing priced?
Three pricing models: per-drawing rates (typically 40–55% below US/AU onshore rates), fixed-scope project pricing for defined packages, or monthly retainer for managed-service engagements with guaranteed capacity. Use our cost calculator for a benchmark.
Can you work to our ISO 19650 BEP?
Yes. We build to your BIM Execution Plan including LOD requirements, model origin, shared coordinates, worksets, and file naming. If you don't have a BEP, we can help draft one aligned to ISO 19650.
Do you support BIM 360 and ProjectWise integration?
Yes. We work inside Autodesk Construction Cloud / BIM 360, Bentley ProjectWise, and client-hosted Citrix / VDI environments. Our onshore PMs run weekly coordination meetings alongside your internal team.
What turnaround can I expect on a BIM pilot?
Pilot models (one discipline, small area) deliver in 2–4 weeks. Full multi-discipline packages scale from there based on project size and clash-resolution iterations. Revision SLA is 24 hours on managed-service engagements.
What's the difference between this and the BIM / 3D service page?
This page is the commercial landing for BIM outsourcing specifically — the discipline-buy conversation. Our BIM / 3D services page covers the full range of BIM capabilities in more depth including specific case studies.
