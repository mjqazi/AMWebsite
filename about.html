Our Story

We started because the outsourcing market was broken.

When Advanced Mechanix was founded in 2020, the offshore drafting market was split between generalist body-shops with no domain knowledge and boutique shops that couldn't scale past a handful of drawings. Engineering managers were stuck choosing between quality and capacity.

We built a third option: certified discipline specialists, onshore project management, multi-stage QA against jurisdiction-specific codes, and a first-drawing-free guarantee so clients never pay for documentation they can't use. Five years later, we've delivered 300+ projects across North America, Australia, Europe, and Asia.

Mission

Documentation isn't a compliance exercise.

Our mission is to deliver engineering documentation that actually performs: drawings that pass plan-check first time, models that survive coordination, analytics that operators trust. That requires specialists who care about the craft — not generalists chasing the next ticket.

We measure success on three dimensions: did the documentation hold up in the field, did we meet the committed timeline, and did the client want to work with us again. We track every project against all three. That's why 98% of clients renew.
