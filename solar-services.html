FAQ

Questions solar EPC operations leaders ask us

What AHJs have you worked with?
We have prepared permit packages for over 200 AHJs across California, Texas, Arizona, New York, Florida, and Australia. We maintain a per-AHJ checklist of local quirks, utility interconnection forms, and plan-check requirements.
Do you prepare NEC 2023 compliant packages?
Yes. All packages are prepared to NEC 2023 Article 690 requirements plus applicable IEEE 1547 interconnection, UL 1741 SA inverter, and local ASCE 7-22 wind/snow load standards.
What is your turnaround on a standard commercial permit package?
Most commercial rooftop packages (under 500 kW) are delivered in 5–7 business days from receipt of site survey and BOM. Ground mount and utility-scale packages run 2–4 weeks depending on complexity.
Can you handle utility interconnection applications?
Yes. We prepare utility interconnection applications, supplemental review packages, and respond to utility technical queries as part of our managed service engagement.
Do you do residential as well as commercial?
Our primary focus is commercial, industrial, and utility-scale solar. We take on residential volumes (50+ permits/month) under managed service agreements but do not accept one-off residential permits.
