§01 — Background

Building the drafting firm the offshore market needed.

Junaid founded Advanced Mechanix in 2020 after years of seeing engineering managers forced to choose between onshore cost and offshore quality. The gap, he recognised, wasn't talent — it was operational rigor: no NDAs, no QA gates, no named leadership, no code verification.

He built Advanced Mechanix on the opposite premise: every engagement starts with a mutual NDA, every deliverable passes multi-stage QA against jurisdiction-specific codes, and every account has a named senior engineer accountable for quality. The first drawing is always free, so clients never pay for documentation they can't use.

Five years later, Advanced Mechanix serves 300+ clients across North America, Australia, Europe, and Asia, including tier-1 mining operators and major engineering consultancies. Junaid continues to run client relationships personally for every new engagement.
