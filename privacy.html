Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Effective date: April 2026 · Last updated: April 2026

Who we are

Advanced Mechanix ("we", "us", "our") is a specialized engineering documentation firm registered in California, USA. Our office address is 12478 San Bruno Cove, San Diego, CA 92130, USA. Contact: company@advancedmechanix.com.

What information we collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide through our contact and consultation forms: name, email address, company name, phone number, and any project details you choose to share.

We automatically collect technical information through standard web analytics: IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, referring URL, and session duration.

How we use information

  • To respond to consultation and contact requests
  • To deliver requested resources (guides, calculators, sample drawings)
  • To understand how visitors use our site and improve it
  • To send project-related communication during active engagements
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Cookies and analytics

Our site uses cookies for essential functionality and anonymized analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings without losing access to the site's content. We use Google Analytics 4 for aggregated usage analytics; Google's data practices are governed by their privacy policy.

Your rights

If you are located in the EU / EEA (GDPR), UK (UK GDPR), or California (CCPA/CPRA), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Object to or restrict our processing
  • Request data portability
  • Opt out of sale or sharing (CCPA/CPRA)
  • Lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority

To exercise any right, email privacy@advancedmechanix.com. We respond within 30 days.

Data security & retention

We use encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest where applicable, role-segregated access controls, and audit-logged activity. Personal information from contact forms is retained for 24 months after last contact unless a longer retention is required by contract or law.

International transfers

Some of our production team operates from Pakistan. Personal information may be processed outside the jurisdiction where it was collected. Where required, we use Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards for cross-border transfers.

Children

Our services are directed at businesses and not at individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We update this policy as needed. Material changes will be announced at the top of this page with at least 30 days' notice before they take effect.
